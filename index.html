Lost and Found

Site Purpose:

Our mission is to bridge the gap between lost belongings and the people who miss them. By leveraging the power of community, honesty, and smart technology, we aim to transform the stress of losing an item into a story of human kindness and reunion.

Site Pages:

  • The Lost Items page allows good Samaritans, retail staff, or public transit workers to browse to see if an object that they just stumbled across matches a report filed by a distressed owner.
  • The Found Items page allows people who have lost something to browse or filter through recently discovered items.
  • The Help / Contact Us page provides email addresses so users can reach support staff for manual intervention.
  • The About Us page introduces the team.

How to use this site:

  • If you lost something, go to the Lost Items page and check to see if anyone has posted your item there. If you can't find it, then fill out the Lost Item Form to post a report of your own.
  • If you found something, go to the Found Items page and check to see if the item that you found matches an item someone else lost. If you can't find a match, fill out the Found Item Form to post a report of the item that you found.
